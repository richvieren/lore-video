Apex OS
Content · Ads · Brand · 2026

Apex OS is a personal training program for men who build companies and neglect their body while doing it. Founders, entrepreneurs, high achievers. The kind of people who'll optimise their ad spend to the decimal but haven't trained consistently in months.

Lore produces all of Apex's video content. Founder-led talking heads, ad creatives, training footage, short-form cuts for Instagram and TikTok. The content runs through the founders' personal accounts because that's where the trust lives. Every video is built to feel like a conversation with someone who actually trains, not a gym brand selling memberships.
